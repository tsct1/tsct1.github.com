About

Medical Simulation Center of Siberian State University was founded in 2011 to teach and train specialists in the field of obstetrics and gynecology, neonatology, anesthesiology and intensive care (Resolution of the Government of Russian Federation dated 31.12.2010 �1220).

In 2014 Certification Laboratory became a department of Medical Simulation Center (Order number 10 on 15/01/2014).

The center is equipped with the latest educational technology tools, interactive computer equipments, patient simulators, skills trainers, CPR manikins, ALS manikins, AED trainers ITLS manikins, real medical equipment and tools, especially for resuscitation, intensive care and first aid.

This site was created for the online-support courses conducted by Simulation Center of Siberian State Medical University. In order to access learning materials login with your account name
